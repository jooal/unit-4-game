THE FRUIT GAME!

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four fruit below. By clicking on a fruit you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number given. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each fruit is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the value of each fruit changes.
